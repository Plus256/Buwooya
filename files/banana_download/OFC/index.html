OUR MISSION

We Do a Lot To Make the World Better!

LETS PLAY:In this area of our programs we are passionate about making lasting change by helping children reach their full potential by healing emotional traumas through Play Therapy. We know that the children’s care -takers are key in the healthy development of the children, so we provide a number of different education programs to support staff working with vulnerable and orphan children

LETs LOVE:

The goal of this program is to identify "at risk" families in Uganda where children are at risk of becoming orphans due to poverty. In this program we change stories and provide hope and love where there was none. We achieve this through supporting a select number of families, providing them with support to break the poverty cycle and encourage them into a brighter future. who we are loving (this will have the stories of the families we are helping) .The Allen Family .Christine .Annett
